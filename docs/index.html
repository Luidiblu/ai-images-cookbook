Purpose

The incredible ability of artificial intelligence to generate images from data is something that has been impressing more and more.
This power has the potential to revolutionize various sectors, and we are only at the beginning of this journey.

My research focuses on exploring this potential in the area of images. Documentation is essential for any type of research and serves as a record of discoveries, in addition to allowing others to use artificial intelligence to generate images from data.

With the advancement of technology, I hope we can discover more and more about the benefits of artificial intelligence in images and see its use spread throughout all sectors of society.

Dall·E 2

DALL·E 2 has learned the relationship between images and the text used to describe them. It uses a process called “diffusion,” which starts with a pattern of random dots and gradually alters that pattern towards an image when it recognizes specific aspects of that image.
